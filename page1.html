This month's featured artist: MONET





Claude Monet was a pioneer of the impressionist movement in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The Paris born artist is well known for his nature landscape painting - the most famous of which focus on waterlilies. His painting style is very ethereal and perfectly captures a suggestion of movement in his compositions. We are most privileged to be exhibiting some of his pieces for a special exhibition in early 2019 to celebrate his impact on contemporary art today.
